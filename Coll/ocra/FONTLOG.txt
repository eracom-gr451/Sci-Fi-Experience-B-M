  OCR-A Font Information
  
  The OCR-A font was developed by the American National 
  Standards Institute (ANSI) to be readable by the computers 
  of the 1960s. The OCR-A font is still used commercially in 
  payment advice forms so that a lockbox company can 
  determine the account number and amount owed on a bill when 
  processing a payment.
